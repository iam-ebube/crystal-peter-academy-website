About Us

Welcome to Crystal Peter Academy – Nurturing Young Brains Since 2020 Crystal Peter Academy is more than just a school — it is a home for excellence, growth, and transformation. Since its founding 5 years ago, our academy has been deeply committed to raising well-rounded learners who are prepared for the challenges of tomorrow. A Track Record of Excellence Founded in 2020 By Mrs Peter Nkechi Onyewsi, Crystal Peter Academy has quickly grown to become one of the most respected educational institutions in Ogun State. With a strong focus on academic excellence, character development, and modern teaching methods, we have consistently produced students who not only excel in the classroom but also demonstrate leadership and integrity in their communities. Our school combines traditional values with 21st-century learning tools, giving our students the best of both worlds. Our dedicated team of teachers and staff work tirelessly to ensure that every child receives the attention, support, and resources needed to thrive. Experienced and Passionate Educators Modern Classrooms and Learning Aids Clean, Safe, and Child-Friendly Environment Moral and Character Education Creative Arts, ICT, and Sports Programs Affordable School Fees with High-Quality Education Growing With You In just five years, we have seen amazing progress — not only in the number of students we serve but also in the impact we make. Our students have gone on to win academic awards, represent the school in external competitions, and shine in their secondary school entrance exams. At Crystal Peter Academy, we believe every child is a star waiting to shine. We are proud of how far we have come and even more excited about the future.
